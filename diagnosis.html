    What Happens After Diagnosis?

    Based on the patient's health, preferences, and cancer stage, doctors evaluate test results and create a treatment plan. Options and support are thoroughly covered.

    Treatment Options

    Explore common approaches for treating prostate conditions.

    Surgery

    Often used in early stages. Involves removing part or all of the prostate gland.

    Radiation Therapy

    High-energy rays used to destroy cancer cells. Can be external or internal.

    Hormone Therapy

    Lowers the levels of hormones that fuel cancer growth.

    Supportive Care

    Focuses on improving quality of life and managing side effects.
